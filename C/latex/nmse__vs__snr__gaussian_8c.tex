\subsection{Detailed Description}
C routine that generates the data for building the Figure 2(a). 

Figure 2(a) depicts the curves of N\+M\+SE in dB of the recovered coefficient vector versus S\+NR in dB of the random projections when the additive noise follows a Gaussian distribution. This curves are obtained by applying the proposed reconstruction algorithm and the weighted median hard-\/thresholding algorithm.
