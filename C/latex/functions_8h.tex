Returns a random number following a $\varepsilon$-\/contaminated noise model. More precisely, the $\varepsilon$-\/contaminated distribution consists in a mixed noise model that merges Gaussian random samples and sparse gross errors. In other words, the $\varepsilon$-\/contaminated noise model follows the model given by \[ f_{\eta}(\eta) = \varepsilon \mathcal{N}(0,\sigma^2) + (1-\varepsilon)\mathcal{N}(0,\kappa \sigma^2), \] where $ \varepsilon$ is the impulsiveness level for the mixed noise model; $ \sigma^2 $ is the variance of the Gaussian noise; and $\kappa $ is the ratio between the variance of the impulsive noise and the variance of the Gaussian noise. \begin{DoxyReturn}{Returns}
